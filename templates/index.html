How to use:

  • Put the stock tickers you want to calculate the efficient frontier on the cells above.
  • The tickers need to be the same as in Yahoo Finance website.

  • Markowitz efficient frontier measure the "quality" of your return based on how much risk you incur
  • This application exhibit how much to allocate in each asset as percentage of your total portfolio
  • The percentage of assets fluctuate over time, needing to rebalance the portfolio
  • The correlation of risk between the stocks in portfolio go to zero if well done, but could go to median variance on the other way, be aware. You can use variety of different assets class to have better diversified your risk.
  • For this model we are using a risk free rate of 3% to calculate Sharpe ratio
